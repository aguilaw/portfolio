Agendi

Logo Design

2015

Agendi.ninja is an upcoming web-app that will facilitate the registration process for students everywhere. Signing up for classes every quarter will go from a frustating chore to a simple and quick process

The logo is flat yet impactful and memorable. The Logotype and mascot share a unique sharp and geometric design that allows them to effectively represent the app when used in unison or individually

Process

The initial ideas for agendi centered aroung futuristic technology, but after the domain was secured there was an obvious change of direction.

hand drawn sketches were refined in illustrator
